OUR STORY

Fifteen Years of
Musical Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a cornerstone of KNUST's cultural and spiritual life

✦
2011
Foundation
NUAS Choir KNUST established with 15 founding members and a burning vision for musical excellence and praise on the KNUST campus.
♪
2013
First Major Concert
Performed our inaugural full-length concert to a packed audience, announcing to the campus that something extraordinary had begun.
★
2016
National Recognition
Received national recognition at the Ghana Universities Choir Competition, putting NUAS Choir on the national choral map for the very first time.
✝
2019
10th Anniversary Concert
Celebrated a decade of music at ATCAC-KNUST, reuniting over 80 alumni members for a historic evening that moved the entire congregation to tears.
◈
2022
Alumni Network
Established the NUAS Choir Alumni Association with over 200 former members, ensuring our community endures long beyond graduation.
✦
2026
LAUS DEO '26
The culmination of fifteen magnificent years — NUAS Choir returns to Archbishop Thomas Cranmer Anglican Church for a once-in-a-generation anniversary concert. Praise be to God.
